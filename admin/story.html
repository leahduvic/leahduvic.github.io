Story of my life... (sort of)

Glimpse into the past..

In my life, there has always been a passion for creating, expressing and consistent learning. All of my hobbies have been framed around these three things. As a child, I found myself enamered by music with all it's intricacies to constructing legos to create whatever popped into my head. Once I began college, I found myself on the same path only I went into a different media form of film photography. The ambiguity and constant learning of new techniques made for a frustrating, yet a thriving learning environment for myself. During my teenage years, I found myself mesmerized and interested in computers and the way they process.

More recent years..

In recent years, living paycheck to paycheck working retail and taking care of other peoples lives, I decided it was time to take care of myself. I revisited coding and found that I do enjoy it. The next steps led me to how will I make this happen, where can I do this, and how long would it take me to complete it. After substantial googling and a friends recommendation, I found NSS- Nashville Software School. Every thing lined up in my life, personally and financially, to make this happened. So I jumped at the opportunity to improve my life and have a career I could be proud of.
