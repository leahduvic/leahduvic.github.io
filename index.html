About

For as long as I can remember, there has always been a passion for creating, expressing and constant learning. All the hobbies I acquired have been framed around these three things. As a child, I found myself enamered by music with all its intricacies, constructing legos to create whatever popped into my head, and creating all forms of art. In my teenage years, I found myself mesmerized and interested in computers and all of their interworkings. I was curious how these devices could impact our lives in so many ways. Then came college, I knew I wanted to use my more creative side and be challenged, after trying a few other paths for degree options. Being that I love to create and challenge myself I chose a different media form in studio art concentrating on black and white photography using film. The ambiguity and constant learning of new techniques made for a frustrating, yet a thriving learning environment for myself.
In recent years, living from paycheck to paycheck working retail and taking care of other peoples lives, I decided it was time to take care of myself. I revisited coding and found that spark of curiosity from my teenage years to only discover how much I enjoy it. The next steps led me to how will I make this happen, where can I do this, and how long would it take me to complete it. After substantial googling and a friends recommendation, I found NSS- Nashville Software School. Every thing lined up in my life, personally and financially, to make this happen. So I jumped at the opportunity to improve my life, to challenge myself and have a career I could be proud of.
